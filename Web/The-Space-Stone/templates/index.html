The Space Stone

click

The Space Stone, also known as the Tesseract, is one of the six Infinity Stones, the remnant of a singularity that predates the universe. It has the ability to create portals from one part of the universe to another and can also provide the user with vast energy.

Key Facts:

  • Color: Blue
  • First Appearance: Captain America: The First Avenger
  • Possessed By: Red Skull, S.H.I.E.L.D., Loki, Thanos
  • Abilities: Space manipulation, teleportation, energy projection
