The Time Stone

The Time Stone, also known as the Eye of Agamotto, is one of the six Infinity Stones, the remnant of a singularity that predates the universe. It has the ability to manipulate time, allowing the user to see into the past and future, freeze time, and even alter events.

Key Facts:

  • Color: Green
  • First Appearance: Doctor Strange
  • Possessed By: Agamotto, Ancient One, Doctor Strange, Thanos
  • Abilities: Time manipulation, time travel, foresight
